A little bit about me...

I hold a B.A. in Theater and an M.A. in Film Studies. While I have been able to apply the critical thinking skills I honed in school to my various roles in Digital and Media Asset Management, I have never truly had the opportunity to tap into the creative urge that drew me to theater and film in the first place. I began to investigate a career transition into the tech industry and as soon as I learned my first simple HTML and CSS commands...I was hooked. I had finally found it!

That beautiful blend of rules and structure and wonderful “nerdy” best practices plus the creative outlet I was searching for. This is my passion. Learning and practicing and growing in my knowledge while continually adding new pieces to the puzzle. I've since added command line and Git/Github skills and plan on building up my portfolio in responsive web design. I'm now on the path to making a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organizational skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
